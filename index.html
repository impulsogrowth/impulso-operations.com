Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How fast will I see results? +

Most clients have leads in their pipeline within 48 hours of onboarding. First booked call typically happens within the first week of outreach going live.

Do I need to be tech-savvy? +

Not at all. Everything runs through a simple interface. If you can send a text message, you can use this system. We handle all the technical setup.

What makes this different from other agencies? +

We don't just run ads. We build you a fully automated system — lead hunting, outreach, follow-up, content, and reporting — all AI-powered and running 24/7 without you lifting a finger.

Is there a contract or commitment? +

No long-term contracts. Month-to-month only. We keep clients because of results, not paperwork. Give us 30 days notice to cancel — no penalties, no hidden fees.

What niches do you work with? +

Any local service business — realtors, med spas, contractors, dentists, mortgage brokers, gyms, law firms, restaurants, and more. If you have clients to close, we can build the pipeline.

Can I license the system for my own agency? +

Yes. We offer white-label licensing for agency owners who want to offer this system under their own brand. Setup fee + monthly license. Book a call to learn more.
